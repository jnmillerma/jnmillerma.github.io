The Researcher Behind the Data

I'm Jazzmyne Miller — a Senior User Researcher with 5+ years in UX research and over 10 years in survey design and quantitative analysis. I've built my career at the intersection of rigorous statistical thinking and deep human understanding.

My path from market research at Mintel to UX research at Meta and PeopleFun gave me something rare: the ability to ask the right qualitative questions and run the advanced statistics to validate what the data means. I don't just surface insights — I prove them.
